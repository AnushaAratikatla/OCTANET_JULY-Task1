About

The College Hackathon is a thrilling event that brings together students from various disciplines to collaborate, learn, and create innovative solutions. Join us for an exciting weekend filled with coding challenges, networking opportunities, and inspiring talks from industry experts.

Steps to Participate

Step 1: Register

Create an account and register for the hackathon on our website. Limited spots available, so don't miss out!

Step 2: Form a Team

Find like-minded individuals and form a team of 3-5 members. Collaboration is the key to success!

Step 3: Prepare

Get ready for the hackathon by brushing up on your coding skills and exploring relevant technologies.

Step 4: Hack Away

During the hackathon, work together with your team to develop an innovative project within the given timeframe.
